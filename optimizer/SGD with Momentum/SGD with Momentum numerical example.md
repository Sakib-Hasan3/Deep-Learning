## Example Setup (Quadratic loss)

‡¶ß‡¶∞‡¶ø ‡¶Ü‡¶Æ‡¶∞‡¶æ ‡¶è‡¶Æ‡¶® ‡¶è‡¶ï‡¶ü‡¶ø function minimize ‡¶ï‡¶∞‡¶§‡ßá ‡¶ö‡¶æ‡¶á:


$$
J(w) = \frac{1}{2}(w-4)^2
$$

‡¶è‡¶∞ gradient:


$$
\frac{dJ}{dw} = w-4
$$


**Initial values**

$$
w_0 = 0
$$
$$
v_0 = 0 \quad \text{(velocity ‡¶∂‡ßÅ‡¶∞‡ßÅ‡¶§‡ßá 0)}
$$
$$
\eta = 0.1
$$
$$
\beta = 0.9
$$

---

## SGD with Momentum formulas


**Velocity update**
$$
v_t = \beta v_{t-1} + \eta \frac{dJ}{dw}
$$


**Weight update**
$$
w \leftarrow w - v_t
$$

---

## Iteration 1


**Gradient**
$$
g_0 = w_0 - 4 = 0 - 4 = -4
$$


**Velocity**
$$
v_1 = 0.9(0) + 0.1(-4) = -0.4
$$


**Update weight**
$$
w_1 = w_0 - v_1 = 0 - (-0.4) = 0.4
$$


**Loss**
$$
J(w_0) = \tfrac12(0-4)^2 = 8
$$
$$
J(w_1) = \tfrac12(0.4-4)^2 = \tfrac12( -3.6)^2 = 6.48
$$

---

## Iteration 2


**Gradient**
$$
g_1 = w_1 - 4 = 0.4 - 4 = -3.6
$$


**Velocity**
$$
v_2 = 0.9(-0.4) + 0.1(-3.6) = -0.36 - 0.36 = -0.72
$$


**Update weight**
$$
w_2 = w_1 - v_2 = 0.4 - (-0.72) = 1.12
$$


**Loss**
$$
J(w_2) = \tfrac12(1.12-4)^2 = \tfrac12(-2.88)^2 = 4.1472
$$

---

## Iteration 3


**Gradient**
$$
g_2 = w_2 - 4 = 1.12 - 4 = -2.88
$$


**Velocity**
$$
v_3 = 0.9(-0.72) + 0.1(-2.88) = -0.648 - 0.288 = -0.936
$$


**Update weight**
$$
w_3 = w_2 - v_3 = 1.12 - (-0.936) = 2.056
$$


**Loss**
$$
J(w_3) = \tfrac12(2.056-4)^2 = \tfrac12(-1.944)^2 \approx 1.889
$$
